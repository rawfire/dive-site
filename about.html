Menu

Serving all afternoon and late night drinks including specialty cocktails, shots and frozen drinks. Some seasonal favorites among our customers include Apple Pie on the Rocks, Jack Frost Colada, Peppermint Patty, Hot Vine, Whiskey Business, Steamy Apple and countless others. Try our non-alcoholic cocktails such as the Berry Mint Lemonade and East Villager in addition to our non-alcoholic beer of the week.

Our draft beers rotate with the seasons and currently feature Stella Artois, Montauk Wavechaser IPA, Blue Moon and a top rated Founders Brown Ale. Bottled beers include Corona, Heineken, Guinness, Coors Light and McKenzie’s Winter Crisp Cider. Canned beverages include an All Day IPA, White Claw, Tiny Juicy IPA and Fancy Papers from Cigar City Brewing, among others. We have various Red, White, Rosé, Sparkling and Sangria Wines ranging from house to upscale brands from the most infamous wine regions across the globe.

Our Famous Fries and 20 types of sauces.

We handcut all fries and use different frying methods for Vegan, and Glutten Options. Our 20 Types of sauces come from all over the world. Our owners traveled all over the world looking and curating the list of sauces to accompany your Fries come and join us!

Our Style of service

We serve a casual style of finger food with a high quality of service from the simple dish to the best mixologist in NYC come and join our familiar and cozy corner of the city.
